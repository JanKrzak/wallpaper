# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 2026_

Wallpaper Backgrounds respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the Wallpaper Backgrounds iOS app.

## Information We Collect

### Usage Data
We collect limited usage data to understand how the app is used. This includes information such as:
- App interactions
- Screens viewed
- Feature usage

This data is used only for analytics, app improvement, and ensuring proper functionality.

### User Content
If you choose to upload content, the app may collect:
- Images (wallpapers)
- Descriptions or metadata provided by the user

This content is used solely to provide app features such as browsing, filtering, and community sharing.

### Device Identifiers
The app may collect a pseudonymous device identifier to:
- Enable app functionality
- Prevent abuse
- Support analytics

This identifier is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking across apps or websites.

## How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:
- App functionality
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Content personalization within the app

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Third-Party Services

Wallpaper Backgrounds uses trusted third-party services that may collect limited data to provide their functionality:
- Analytics services (e.g. Firebase Analytics)
- Backend services for storing user-generated content
- Advertising services to display in-app ads

These services are used in compliance with Apple App Store privacy guidelines.

## Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to operate and improve the app.

## Your Choices

- You can use the app without creating an account
- You can choose whether to upload content
- You can stop using the app at any time
