# Wallpaper Backgrounds

Wallpaper Backgrounds is an iOS app that helps users discover, filter, and save beautiful wallpapers for their phones. Browse a growing gallery of high-quality backgrounds in HD and 4K, explore categories, and personalize your device with ease.

## Features

- Explore a large collection of wallpapers
- Categories and styles (Nature, Space, Anime, Minimal, Cyberpunk, and more)
- Smart filters to quickly find the right wallpaper
- Trending and popular wallpapers from the community
- Fullscreen preview before saving
- Save wallpapers in high quality (HD & 4K)
- Clean, modern, and fast user interface

## Privacy

Wallpaper Backgrounds is designed with privacy in mind.

- No user accounts required
- No personal contact information collected
- No location tracking
- No cross-app or third-party tracking

The app collects limited usage data and user-generated content strictly for:
- App functionality
- Analytics
- Content personalization

All data collection complies with Apple App Store privacy guidelines.
